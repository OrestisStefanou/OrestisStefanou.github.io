Your AI Investment Advisor

Get personalized, data-backed investment guidance through natural conversation. Powered by Claude, GPT, or Gemini.

Get Started Read the Blog

Financial advice is either expensive, biased, or generic. InvestPal combines real-time market data with AI that remembers your goals, risk tolerance, and portfolio — delivering professional-grade guidance in a simple conversation.

What InvestPal Does

Everything you need for informed investment decisions, in one conversation.

Real-Time Market Research

Ask about any stock, ETF, crypto, commodity, or economic indicator and get current, data-backed answers from trusted sources like Alpha Vantage and CoinGecko.

Personalized to You

InvestPal adapts to your risk tolerance, knowledge level, and financial goals. It builds a profile over time and remembers your preferences across conversations.

Smart Reminders

Mention an earnings date or a rebalancing plan, and InvestPal tracks it. It proactively reminds you when it's time to act — no follow-up falls through the cracks.

Portfolio-Aware

Optionally connect your Alpaca (stocks) or Coinbase (crypto) account for advice that accounts for your actual positions, allocations, and unrealized gains.

How It Works

Three steps. No setup required.

1

Ask Anything

Type a question in plain language: "Should I increase my tech exposure?" or "What are Buffett's latest moves?"

2

AI Researches

InvestPal pulls real-time data from markets, analyzes your profile, checks your portfolio, and cross-references institutional moves.

3

Get Actionable Guidance

Receive personalized, data-backed advice with full context. InvestPal remembers the conversation and follows up.
